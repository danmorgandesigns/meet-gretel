iCloud Sync

Seamless Personal Device Integration

Sync your field data automatically across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account. Start data collection on your iPhone in the field, review and refine on your iPad or Apple Silicon Mac — all with the same up-to-date information. No additional cloud services or IT infrastructure required for individual workflow management.

How iCloud Sync Works

iCloud sync automatically keeps your app data synchronized across all devices signed into the same iCloud account.

What Gets Synced

  • Routes and trails you've recorded
  • Points of Interest (POls) you've saved
  • Custom POl categories you've created

Requirements

  • Signed into iCloud on all devices
  • Internet connection for syncing
  • Sufficient iCloud storage

Privacy & Security

Your data is encrypted and stored securely in your personal iCloud account. Only devices signed into your iCloud account can access this data.

Conflict Resolution

If the same item is modified on multiple devices, the app will keep both versions to prevent data loss. You can manually resolve conflicts by choosing which version to keep.
